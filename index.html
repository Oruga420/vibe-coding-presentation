02 / THE MENTAL STACK

The Mental
Framework.

  1. 01

    Meta — Define success first

    Before typing anything, decide what a perfect output looks like. "Build me a website" is weak. "Build a SaaS landing page with dark mode, pricing tiers, and a waitlist form" is powerful.

  2. 02

    Context — Feed it everything

    Upload your docs, brand guidelines, existing code, and references. The more context the AI has, the better the output. This is where Google AI Studio's 2M token window shines.

  3. 03

    Rules — Set boundaries

    "Use TypeScript." "Follow REST conventions." "No inline styles." Rules prevent hallucinations and keep the AI on track. Think of them as guardrails, not limitations.

  4. 04

    Examples — Show, don't tell

    Include 2-3 examples of what you want. Paste a JSON sample, a code snippet, or a paragraph in your desired style. Examples are the strongest signal you can give an AI.

  5. 05

    Request — Pull the trigger

    Only now make your actual ask. Be direct and actionable: "Generate the React component for the pricing section based on the above context, rules, and examples."
